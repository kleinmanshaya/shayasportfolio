Book covers and editorial design for a literary series.

Book Design & Editorial

Kis Library is a pocket-sized series built around a single idea: short books about what it means to be human. The design had to feel personal without being precious, approachable enough to carry in your pocket, considered enough to stay on your shelf.

The visual language draws from classic literary publishing: restrained typography, quiet color, and covers that give each story its own identity while clearly belonging to the same family.
