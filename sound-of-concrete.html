A raw poster campaign built on texture, contrast, and the visual grammar of brutalism.

Poster Campaign

Tel Aviv is a city made of concrete and cinema. This campaign started as a personal experiment, an attempt to translate that feeling into print.

Three posters. Raw texture, heavy type, and the visual logic of brutalism applied to a film context. No brief, no client, just a question: what does the city sound like when you turn it into a poster?
